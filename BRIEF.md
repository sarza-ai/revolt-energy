# ReVolt Energy — Site Brief

## What we're building
A cinematic, dark-mode marketing site for **ReVolt Energy Group**: decentralised clean-energy infrastructure with BESS at the core for Australia's NEM.

## Confirmed business model (from revolt.au + public materials)
- **Build, own, operate** scalable decentralised energy infrastructure
- **BESS-first**: generate, store, monetise power at the edge
- **Suite**: Generation · Storage · Trading · AI orchestration / Infrastructure OS
- **Partners**: EPC, property owners, C&I, airports, data centres, councils
- **Public narrative also includes** second-life EV batteries into high-capacity BESS / VPP-style networks (LinkedIn)

## Audience / CTAs
- **Dual primary CTAs**: Partner/host a node + Investor inquiry
- Multi-step contact form routes by path

## Design direction
- Deep navy + electric teal/green glows
- Hero: particles (R3F), Australia map with pulsing nodes
- Horizontal scroll solutions (GSAP), Lenis smooth scroll, micro-interactions
- Max spectacle first; degrade with `prefers-reduced-motion` where wired

## Stack
Next.js App Router · TypeScript · Tailwind v4 · Framer Motion · GSAP · Lenis · R3F · Lucide · Sonner

## Out of scope (for later)
- Live CRM/email backend for form
- Real asset telemetry
- CMS for blog body copy
- Production brand assets / legal pages
